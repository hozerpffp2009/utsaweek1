Joseph Arocha bio

Joseph was born and raised in San Antonio back in 1987. He attended elemantary school at WestWood Terrace elementary school. He attended Anson Jones middle school and graduated from John Jay high school.

After high school, Joseph worked odd jobs till his mid 20's. Thats when he found a career in the plumbing industry.

Joseph started out as a plumber apprentice for 2 years. After which he was eligible to take his plumbing test to become a tradesman plumber. He ran service calls for 4 years before he got promoted to a plumbing repair specialist.
